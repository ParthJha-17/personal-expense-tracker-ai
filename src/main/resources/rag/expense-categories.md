# Expense categorization guidance

## FOOD
Restaurants, cafes, fast food, delivery, and dining-related subscriptions.

## GROCERIES
Supermarket purchases, pantry items, household groceries, and bulk food stores.

## TRAVEL
Flights, hotels, rideshare, public transit, gas, tolls, and travel booking fees.

## RENT
Monthly rent, lease payments, and housing-related base payments.

## HEALTH
Pharmacy purchases, clinics, medical bills, dental, vision, and wellness treatments.

## INVESTMENT
Brokerage deposits, stock purchases, crypto buys, and investment platform fees.

## SAVINGS
Transfers into savings accounts, emergency funds, or sinking funds.

## MISC
Everything else that does not clearly match another category.
